What is this?

Well, we wanted to do something fun with our big library of locations. So? We designed a competition for you. While you won't need to find the locations(that's our job afterall), you will be going to locations, and taking photos.

So what do I need to do?

It's simple really! Go to either one, two or all three of the locations, snap pic or three and send them to us! You'll have three days to snap a pic, then we'll close to submissions and post them. For the next three days you'll get to vote for the pics, and whoever has the most photos at the end of the three days will win.

And now what?

Well, that's easy! Go out, snap a pic and send it right to us through instagram, facebook, twitter, or email! You have three days to do so, and we look forward to seeing your submissions!
