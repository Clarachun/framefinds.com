About Us

Our Idea
Frame Finds would be the website that provides young adults and aspiring photographers the ability to find and share picture-perfect locations in Los Angeles and Santa Monica. Once a user has an account, they would be able to favourite, comment, and save the best photo destinations.

Mission Statement
Our mission is to encourage others to explore their surrounding world through photography, discovery, and adventure.

Why We Chose This Idea

With the popularity of Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter and other social media today, picture posting has become something that people do everyday. People are constantly trying to find new places to take pictures and impress their friends or get more followers. FrameFinds was an idea thought up by a team of six remarkable high school girls attending school, or living, in Santa Monica. It was inspired by the two dynamic, exuberant, and vivacious cities we each have close to our hearts and the wish of capturing the special places in both: Santa Monica and Los Angeles. In Santa Monica and Los Angeles alone, there are hundreds of picture perfect places to take photos. We wanted to help others discover them, thus FrameFinds was made. Now teens, photographers, and anyone who values taking the perfect picture will know of all the great picturesque places.

Little about us
We are a team of 14-18 year old girls who started our business though the Santa Monica Youth Tech Program. A program that helps equip high schoolers with the abilities to begin a startup of their own. After much debating of what our idea should be all throughout of the second week, we came to a desicion around our third week. Deciding that we were going to begin a startup of an activity we all love. Photography. Hope you like our website!
