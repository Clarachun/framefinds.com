The romance of the past.

For over a century, The Revy proudly served the Royal Australian Navy. Today, the visible traces of this rich heritage are preserved.

This Darling Island wharf echoes with stories that draw deeply on the city’s identity.

Evoking the past—yet resolutely pointing to a glorious future—these exclusive residences will redefine waterfront living.

A new golden age.

From its royally endorsed name to the sovereign initials on its façade, this Sydney landmark is set to become the city’s most distinctive address.

The Revy’s eight storeys comprise just 46 residences, featuring a range of layouts with two, three or four large bedrooms, including the stately penthouses.

Thanks to the building’s orientation and unusual footprint, each home enjoys east and west aspects—an open plan that welcomes the sun’s golden rays, from daybreak till evening.
